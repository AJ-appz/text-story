Texting Story Maker Support

Welcome to the Texting Story Maker support page. Here you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get in touch with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Texting Story Maker?

Texting Story Maker is a creative app that lets you make fictional chat-based stories, add avatars, and export them as videos or screenshots.

2. Does Texting Story Maker store any of my data?

No. We do not collect or store any of your data. Everything stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

3. Does the app use AI or external servers?

No. All functionality is local. We do not use AI-generated content or process your data on external servers.

4. Why does the app request media access?

Media access is only requested if you choose to save or export your story. We do not upload or transmit your files anywhere.
